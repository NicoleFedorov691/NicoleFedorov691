About Us

Étoile Entertainment brings the beauty and artistry of classical ballet to life at private and corporate events. We work exclusively with professional ballet dancers—artists who have trained and performed at the highest levels—to deliver performances that are moving, elegant and grounded in authentic classical style.

Whether you're planning a wedding, a corporate gathering, a milestone celebration, a fashion event, or an intimate party, our dancers bring a polished, sophisticated presence that enhances the atmosphere and captivates your guests. Each performance is thoughtfully tailored to suit your event—whether it's a beloved excerpt from Swan Lake or The Nutcracker, an LED-lit pas de deux, or a custom creation designed specifically for the occasion.

Our mission is to seamlessly weave the beauty of ballet into your event, creating standout entertainment that elevates the experience and leaves a lasting impression on your guests.

Our Repertoire

  • Iconic Classics — Timeless selections from classical ballets such as Swan Lake, The Nutcracker, The Sleeping Beauty, and Romeo and Juliet.
  • Neoclassical & Contemporary Works — Moody, minimalist, or dramatic pieces that offer an edgier interpretation of classical technique.
  • Custom Creations — Original choreography tailored to your event’s theme, venue, or aesthetic—designed to be as unique as the occasion itself.
  • Atmospheric Appearances — Ambient movement, elegant walk-throughs, or subtle stage moments that add presence without interrupting the flow of your event.
  • Costume & Concept Styling — From traditional tutus to sleek, fashion-forward or LED-lit looks, our visual styling adapts seamlessly to your vision and setting.
